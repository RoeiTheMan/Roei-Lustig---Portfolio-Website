# PROJECT_BRIEF.md — Roei Lustig Portfolio

## What This Is
A personal portfolio website for an AI content creator. The site should feel modern, professional, slick, and elegant. All creative design decisions — colors, fonts, layout, spacing, visual style — are yours to decide based on those four words.

---

## Page Structure (index.html)

1. **Nav** — fixed, logo left + links right
2. **Hero** — full viewport, placeholder until showreel is ready. Name + title + CTA buttons.
3. **Stats bar** — 4 metrics: 500M+ organic views, 3+ years experience, 12+ brand projects, and one more of your choice
4. **Selected Work** — project grid with filter bar by category
5. **Concepts callout** — two-column section explaining that concept/self-initiated work is legitimate and built to the same standard as client work
6. **Categories** — 6 areas of work in a grid
7. **About** — photo placeholder left, text right
8. **Services** — grid of what I offer
9. **Contact** — headline + email + social links
10. **Footer**

---

## Project Pages
- Each project gets its own file under `/projects/project-name.html`
- Structure is modular — sections added or removed per project
- Every project page links back to `index.html`

---

## Content & Copy Direction
- Short, confident, sharp copy — no long paragraphs
- Not corporate, not student — premium creative studio tone
- Concept/self-initiated projects are presented as equal to client work
- Categories: Entertainment AI Content, Marketing & Brand Content, Social AI Content, AI Video Concepts, Professional & Corporate AI, Creative Experiments
