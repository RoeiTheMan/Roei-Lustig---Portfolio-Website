LINQ

Direction

LINQ started as a question: could an AI build a world-class interactive SaaS website — scroll animations and all? The answer was yes. What began as a pure build experiment evolved into a full brand exploration. The concept: an AI automation platform that connects your tools and workflows without lifting a finger. Once the product felt real, the brand had to match — premium, minimal, confident. The identity work spans digital and physical: from product UI to billboards, from business cards to building signs. "Workflows that run themselves."
