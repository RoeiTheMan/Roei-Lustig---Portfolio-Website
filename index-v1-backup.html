03  ·  A note from the studio

Concept films are not lesser films.

About a third of what you'll see here was never commissioned. It was made because the image didn't exist yet — to pitch a film, to break a tool, to prove that a frame could hold.

The studio labels self‑initiated work plainly. Concept means nobody paid for it. It does not mean the craft was lighter, the budget smaller, or the result less considered.

Treat concept films as direction reels for the work that's coming next.

05  ·  About

Director's eye.
Prompter's patience.

Roei Lustig is an AI content creator and visual storyteller based between Tel Aviv and Berlin. The studio directs short films, brand work, and concept pieces using generative tools as a camera, a crew, and a darkroom.

He came up in live‑action direction in 2017, then turned fully toward generative workflows in late 2022. The studio treats every frame like a frame — composed, color‑graded, and earned.

Past collaborators include luxury houses, independent musicians, news organizations, and a handful of agencies that prefer the back door. Three to five engagements a year.
