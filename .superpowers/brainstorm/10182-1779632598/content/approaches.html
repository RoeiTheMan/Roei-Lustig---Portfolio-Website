Design approach — how much does Pelago's brand bleed in?

The portfolio is dark (#0a0a0c). Pelago is navy + cream. How do they meet?

A

Portfolio-first — brand through assets only

Full dark theme throughout. No brand color injected. Pelago's identity comes purely through the videos and images. Clean, consistent with every other project page.

portfolio palette, coral accents
B

Brand-tinted — navy bleeds into accents (Recommended)

Dark background stays, but Pelago's navy (#082F63) replaces coral as the accent — section labels, deliverables badge dot, subtle dividers. The page feels like it belongs to Pelago without breaking the portfolio shell.

navy replaces coral as accent
C

Brand section flip — identity images on cream background

The 3 identity images (symbol → logo → packaging) sit on a cream/off-white section — matching how those assets actually look. Rest of the page is dark. One section speaks the brand's own language.

dark page + one cream section
