05  ·  About

Director's eye.
Generative tools.

Roei Lustig is an AI content creator and visual storyteller based in Tel Aviv.

Started in 2023, the moment Midjourney and ChatGPT made it clear that world-class visuals were no longer locked behind a crew and a budget. Jumped in immediately — first with print, stock, and viral social pages, then deeper into content creation, including a full-time role producing content for a large brand in 2025.

Now focused on the commercial and advertising market. Working with brands directly from the first idea through script, production, and delivery. Every frame curated, every detail intentional.

The technology moves fast. The goal is always to be one step ahead of it.
